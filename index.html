WTF is the
Pluriverse?

The pluriverse is a transmedia lorecrafting experiment in collective imagineering.

Pluriverse: A pluriverse is a world in which diverse hopes can be sown, multiple opportunities can be cultivated, and a plurality of meaningful lives can be achieved by the richly different and caring people we are.

Transmedia: Utilizing numerous media forms to elaborate narrative content. The way the story is told is as important as the content of the story.

Lorecraft: Stories that a community tells itself to strengthen internal alignment and cohesion.

Experiment: Following a method, taking risks, venturing into the unknown.

Collective: Written together, for each other.

Imagineering: The implementation of creative ideas in practical form.

Ethereum is/was/will be Pluriversal

The Pluriverse is focused on raising the sanity waterline of the web3 ecosystem.

We understand Ethereum to be a key piece of coordination and regeneration technology that requires high resolution myth weaving and deep fidelity lore questing to persevere the moral attacks of our contemporary moment.

The Pluriverse draws upon diverse influences such as design thinking, ontology, aesthetic theory, cultural studies, and the roots of cypherpunk to prognosticate a hopeful vision of the future.

A viable future requires thinking-feeling beyond a neutral technocratic position, averting the catastrophic metacrisis, avoiding dysoptian solutionism, and dreaming acutely into the techno-imaginative dependencies to come.

Reminder: Ethereum is neutral, but I am not. - Vitalik
